THE WESTGATE ATTACK

THE 80-HOUR TERRORIST ATTACK IN NAIROBI, KENYA, TOOK PLACE BETWEEN THE 21ST AND 24TH SEPTEMBER 2013, RESULTING IN 67 DEATHS AND OVER 175 WOUNDED

THE SOMALI-BASED ISLAMIC TERRORIST GROUP AL-SHABAAB CLAIMED RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE ATTACK

THE UPMARKET WESTGATE SHOPPING MALL WAS A WELL-KNOWN CENTRE OF WESTERN EXPATRIATE LIFE IN NAIROBI. PREVIOUS WARNINGS SUGGESTED THE COMPLEX WAS IN DANGER OF A TERROR ATTACK.

THE ATTACK TOOK PLACE ON A BUSY SATURDAY MORNING, WITH APPROXIMATELY 1,000 PEOPLE INSIDE THE POPULAR MALL. A LOCAL RADIO STATION WAS HOSTING A CHILDREN'S COOKING COMPETITION ON THE ROOF OF THE BUILDING. AS THE ATTACK BEGAN, THE WINNERS WERE ABOUT TO RECEIVE THEIR PRIZES.

AT LEAST 67 PEOPLE ARE KNOWN TO HAVE DIED, INCLUDING CHILDREN AND FOUR ATTACKERS. OVER 175 WERE INJURED.

THE PICTURE THAT HAS EMERGED IN THE AFTERMATH OF THE ATTACK IS THAT OF A WOEFULLY DISORGANISED AND INCOMPETENT RESPONSE FROM KENYAN AUTHORITIES. A CLASH OF EGOS LEFT A HANDFUL OF KENYAN OFFICERS AND ARMED VIGILANTES TO INITIALLY FIGHT HEAVILY ARMED MILITANTS, IN A BID TO RESCUE HUNDREDS OF SHOPPERS.

TWITTER WAS USED AS A MAIN COMMUNICATION CHANNEL BY ALL PARTICIPANTS, INCLUDING AL-SHABAAB AND THE VICTIMS. MAJOR NEWS WEBSITES, TV CHANNELS AND NEWSPAPERS AROUND THE WORLD USED TWITTER AS A PRIMARY SOURCE OF ATTACK-RELATED NEWS, REVEALING A NEW PATTERN OF MASS MEDIA INFORMATION COLLECTION.

FORMED IN 2006, THE STATED GOAL OF AL-SHABAAB (“THE YOUTH” IN ARABIC) IS TO OVERTHROW THE SOMALI GOVERNMENT AND ESTABLISH AN ISLAMIC EMIRATE WITH AN EXTREME VERSION OF SHARIA LAW. IT HAS COMMITTED A TOTAL OF NEARLY 550 TERRORIST ATTACKS ON THE AFRICAN CONTINENT, KILLING MORE THAN 1,600 AND WOUNDING OVER 2,100 PEOPLE.

AL-SHABAAB EXPANDED ITS ACTIVITY INTO NEIGHBOURING STATES SUCH AS KENYA AND ETHIOPIA IN RETALIATION FOR ONGOING MILITARY PRESENCE IN SOMALIA. WESTGATE WAS CHOSEN AS A SYMBOLIC TARGET TO GAIN INTERNATIONAL ATTENTION IN A REVENGE ACT AGAINST THE KENYAN GOVERNMENT.

THE WESTGATE SHOPPING MALL
IT HAS FIVE LEVELS WITH 80 SHOPS, WITH THE LARGEST BEING THE NAKUMATT SUPERMARKET AND PLANET MEDIA CINEMAS. OTHER LARGE TENANTS INCLUDE CLOTHING AND HOME GOODS SHOPS, TWO BANKS, AND THE MILLIONAIRES CASINO LOCATED ON THE SECOND FLOOR. SMALLER RENTAL UNITS INCLUDE RESTAURANTS AND RETAIL SHOPS FOR INTERNATIONAL BRANDS SUCH AS ADIDAS, CONVERSE, FEDEX, AND SAMSUNG. THE MALL IS CURRENTLY OWNED BY A COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE BUSINESS CALLED SONY HOLDING LTD, WHICH IS HEADED BY AN ISRAELI NATIONAL.

AL-SHABAAB IS A SOMALI-BASED ISLAMIC TERRORIST ORGANIZATION THAT CLAIMED RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE WESTGATE ATTACK. IT EMERGED AS THE MILITANT WING OF SOMALIA'S NOW-DEFUNCT ISLAMIC COURTS UNION (ICU) IN 2006, AS IT FOUGHT ETHIOPIAN FORCES WHO HAD ENTERED SOMALIA TO BACK THE WEAK INTERIM GOVERNMENT. DESPITE THE ICU’S DEFEAT BY SOMALI AND ETHIOPIAN FORCES IN 2007, AL-SHABAAB HAS CONTINUED ITS VIOLENT INSURGENCY IN SOUTHERN AND CENTRAL SOMALIA. AL-SHABAAB AND SOME OF ITS AFFILIATES CONTROLLED MOGADISHU, THE SOMALI CAPITAL, AND KISMAYU, A KEY LOGISTICAL HUB FOR THE GROUP, ALONGSIDE MAJOR PORTIONS OF THE SOMALI COUNTRYSIDE BUT SUSTAINED AN AFRICAN UNION MILITARY CAMPAIGN (TO WHICH KENYA CONTRIBUTES TROOPS) IN RECENT YEARS, WHICH HAS WEAKENED THE GROUP’S HOLD ON SOMALIA CONSIDERABLY. HOWEVER, DESPITE ITS WEAKENED STATE IN SOMALIA, THE WESTGATE ATTACK AND ITS FOLLOW-UP ATTACKS ARE A STARK REMINDER THAT AL-SHABAAB STILL REMAINS A POTENT THREAT.

THE NUMBER OF ATTACKS ATTRIBUTED TO THE SOMALI-BASED GROUP HAS INCREASED RAPIDLY FROM 10 IN 2007 TO MORE THAN 200 IN 2012, AND ARE LARGELY CONCENTRATED AGAINST THE SOMALI GOVERNMENT. IN 2012, ALMOST ONE-QUARTER (22.7%) OF THE GROUP’S ATTACKS TOOK PLACE IN KENYA.

THE TARGETS OF THE ATTACKS HAVE VARIED, BUT GENERALLY INCLUDE PRIVATE CITIZENS/PROPERTY, MILITARY, AND GOVERNMENT TARGETS. THEY ARE ALSO OFTEN CHARACTERIZED BY ARMED ASSAULTS THAT LEAD TO EXTENDED CLASHES, OR MASS-CASUALTY BOMBINGS SUCH AS THE JULY 2010 KAMPALA ATTACKS THAT KILLED 76 PEOPLE WATCHING THE 2010 FOOTBALL WORLD CUP FINAL ON TELEVISION.
